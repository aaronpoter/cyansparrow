# Privacy Policy - Cyan Sparrow

**Effective Date:** March 11, 2026

Cyan Sparrow ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and protect your information when you use our app.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

- **Account Information:** Email and password you provide to register and log in via Supabase.

No other personal or device information (such as IP address or usage data) is collected.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your account information solely to:

- Enable registration and login  
- Provide access to your app account

We do not use your information for marketing or tracking purposes.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

- We **do not sell or rent** your personal information  
- All account information is securely stored in **Supabase** and is not shared with third parties for any purpose other than providing the app service  

---

## 4. User Rights

As a user, you can:

- Delete your account at any time via the app  

---

## 5. Data Security

- Account information is securely stored by **Supabase**  
- All communications use HTTPS  
- Passwords are securely hashed and not visible to us  

---

## 6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

- We may update this policy periodically  
- The latest version will always be available on this page
